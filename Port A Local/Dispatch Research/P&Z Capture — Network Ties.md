## Bodies under analysis

The city's organizational chart presents these as separate. The piece's structural argument is that household ties connect them.

1. **City Council** — 7 elected members; primary legislative + procurement-vote body
2. **Planning & Zoning Commission** — 7 members appointed by Council; recommends zoning + development approvals
3. **Charter Review Commission** — 5-member volunteer board, statutorily six-month terms; appointed by Council
4. **Tourism Bureau Board** — 16 seats; receives HOT pass-through (~$10.5M / 2023)
5. **Port of Corpus Christi Authority** — proposed Harbor Island desal; long-running Port Aransas opposition
6. **City regulatory roles** — STR program, real-estate-broker licensing surface, etc.
7. **Private-sector roles tied to city decisions** — vacation-rental operations, brokerage, custom build, restaurant operations, bank lending
