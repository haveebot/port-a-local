## Format rules
- No ALL CAPS (except brand wordmarks: PORT A LOCAL)
- No exclamation points unless genuinely surprising
- No "amazing," "incredible," "must-see," "MUST-TRY"
- No "we're so excited" — show, don't announce
- Two-line hook max — anything longer is a carousel
- Link to the site on every conversion-adjacent post — social is the hook, the site is the outcome
