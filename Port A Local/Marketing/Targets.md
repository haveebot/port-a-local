## When to revise targets
- Miss any target by >30% for 2 weeks in a row → re-evaluate
- Hit any target early → raise it; don't coast
- Major external event (hurricane, festival weekend) → adjust that week, don't skew quarter

## Targets we are explicitly NOT setting
- Follower count above all else. Bought / incentivized followers devalue the trust metric.
- Post frequency above quality. 3 great posts > 10 mediocre.
- "Viral moment" metrics. One lucky Reel doesn't fix the platform.
- Comparison with the Tourism Bureau's numbers. Different job, different measuring stick.
