## Why this matters for them (objection prep)

**"We already have the Facebook event."**
Right — and the Facebook event is doing its job (443 going). This isn't replacing FB. It's the place the FB event can *link to* for parking, schedule, FAQ, and day-of coverage. People who don't use Facebook (older Texans, search-traffic visitors) get the festival on Google instead of nothing.

**"Port Aransas CVB already lists it."**
The CVB calendar is good for discovery. But they're not going to liveblog the day, take photos, run a recap, or write the festival's history. We will.

**"We don't want this to feel commercial."**
That's why we built it. No ads, no sponsors, no upsell. The page never asks anyone to spend money. It mentions the shop because the shop is the host — same as how the South Jetty would mention it in an article.

**"Why are you doing this for free?"**
Because PAL's whole model is: directory + editorial coverage of the island, no paid placements anywhere, ever. Festivals are exactly the kind of thing the site exists to cover. We're trying to earn trust before we earn money — and the way we earn money later (when we do) is on portals like cart rentals and beach setups, not on coverage of someone else's event.

**"What if we want to take it down later?"**
We pull it the same day you ask. Your event, your call.
