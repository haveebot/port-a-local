## Phases (from Collie's plan)
1. **Brand Launch** — weeks 1–2 · establish credibility, explain the platform
2. **Discovery & Engagement** — weeks 3–5 · build habit, drive repeat traffic
3. **Service Conversion** — weeks 4+ · turn traffic into bookings + maintenance requests
4. **Community & Partner Growth** — ongoing · expand through local amplification

## Voice rules (non-negotiable)
See the full brand voice at [theportalocal.com/brand](https://theportalocal.com/brand). Summary:
- Local-first, honest, practical, quietly confident, curated not promotional
- Short and clear · minimal fluff · no hype · no exaggerated claims · no sales tone
- We sound like: a knowledgeable local giving recommendations
- We do NOT sound like: a tourism board, travel blog, or corporate directory

## Posting cadence
- 3–5 posts per week
- Primary format: short-form video (Reels)
- Supporting: carousels + static
- Every post closes the loop — either to the site or to a bookable service
