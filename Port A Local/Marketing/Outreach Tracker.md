## Outreach templates

**To a business asking to cross-promote:**
> Hi {name} — Port A Local's a new directory site for the island. You're already listed at theportalocal.com/{category}/{slug} — no charge, no paid placement, nothing you need to do. Wanted to flag we're doing a {spotlight format} next week and {business} is on the shortlist. If you're up for it, tag us on your next post and we'll reshare.
>
> — The Port A Local

**To a press contact:**
> Hi {name} — I publish Port A Local, a directory + editorial platform for Port Aransas. We just dropped a piece called {title} — {one-line summary}. It touches {angle relevant to their outlet}. Read it at {link}. If it reads like something your audience would care about, we'd welcome a link-back or a follow-up conversation.
>
> — The Port A Local

**For a partner who's already mentioned us unprompted:**
> {Name} — thanks for the shoutout. Dropped you a follow from @theportalocal. If there's an angle on {business} you'd want to see written up on the site, send it over.
>
> — The Port A Local

## Do NOT pitch (yet)
- National travel magazines (Sunset, Afar, Condé Nast) — wait until 40+ heritage pieces + 5+ dispatches ship
- Paid sponsorships / affiliate deals — violates "no paid placements" core messaging
- Any outlet that leads with "best beach town" listicles — off-brand
