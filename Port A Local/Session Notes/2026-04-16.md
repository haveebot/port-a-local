## Printable QR poster route (269ba66)
- New dynamic route **`/print/qr/[slug]`** generates letter-size branded posters with a lighthouse-in-center QR code.
- Two targets live:
  - `home` — general awareness, QR points to theportalocal.com.
  - `sandfest` — QR points to the Texas Sandfest Heritage piece.
- **Tech:**
  - `qrcode` npm package.
  - **Error correction level H** (highest) — survives the center overlay (lighthouse icon on sand-colored circle).
  - Navy-on-sand color scheme, lighthouse icon overlay centered.
  - Letter-size `@page` CSS for printing.
  - `robots: noindex` (not meant to be indexed).
  - Preview bar auto-hides on print.
- **Extending:** add a target by editing the `targets` config map in the route. Pattern works for per-portal posters (`rent`, `beach`, `maintenance`) or per-heritage-piece posters.

## Why
- First physical-world PAL artifact — bridges the site to in-person signage.
- First use: Sandfest weekend signage for the Heritage piece. Potential future uses: portal-specific posters for beach/rent/maintenance, per-business flyers, merch packaging inserts.
