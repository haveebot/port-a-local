### Schema expansion (commit `2962f3c`)

Four new JSON-LD helpers in `src/components/StructuredData.tsx`:

- **FAQPageSchema** — Q/A pairs. Wired on `/essentials` using the 10 section titles as questions + items list joined as answers. Primary target: rich FAQ results for "port aransas ferry," "port aransas beach rules," "port aransas golf cart rules" queries.
- **BreadcrumbListSchema** — ordered crumb trail with URLs. Wired site-wide on the four detail route types: `/[category]/[slug]`, `/history/[slug]`, `/guides/[slug]`, `/dispatch/[slug]`. Google requires this to render the labeled breadcrumb in SERP.
- **ItemListSchema** — ordered list. Wired on `/guides/[slug]` with the guide's matched businesses → Google understands each guide page as a curated list, helps "best X in port aransas" capture.
- **PlaceSchema** — reusable Place/TouristAttraction/LandmarksOrHistoricalBuildings helper. Helper created in this commit, wired in the next.
