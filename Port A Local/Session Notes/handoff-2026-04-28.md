## Key recent decisions (haven't all propagated yet)

- **All severities push, including info-tier community announcements**. Initially gated info out (concern: 9 PM lock-screen pings). Reframed per Winston's "good stuff too" feedback — subscribers tapped Enable BECAUSE they want the call. One-tap unsubscribe handles fatigue.
- **One subscription pool for both alert surfaces**. `/wheelhouse/alerts` (banner) and `/wheelhouse/emergency` (events) push to the same `customer-topic / "emergencies"` topic. Visitors tap once, get the full stream.
- **Personal email out of automation**. `winstonciv@gmail.com` swapped to `admin@theportalocal.com` in DEFAULT_NOTIFY for Winston's wheelhouse role. Personal inbox stays personal.
- **PWA icon source SVG must be Satori-friendly**. Lesson learned the hard way: `rotate(-45)` transforms in `lighthouse-full.svg` were silently dropped by next/og's renderer, shipping solid-navy squares. Inlined a transform-free silhouette in `src/lib/pwaLighthouseSvg.ts`. **Future Claude: never use rotate-transforms in SVGs that pass through ImageResponse.**
- **Login redirect must use full-page nav**. `router.replace()` after cookie-set serves cached RSC; bug bit Winston twice. `window.location.assign(fromPath)` is the bulletproof pattern.
