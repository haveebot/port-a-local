## The headline: the rentals tool (`/wheelhouse/rentals`)

The thing Winston sketched — "a calendar DB that records all rentals + dates, live in the wheelhouse, with buttons to fire a vendor update without having to review." Built in 3 phases:

1. **`cart_bookings` table** (`src/data/cart-booking-store.ts`) — the cart twin of `beach_booking_claims`. One row per reservation: customer, dates, cart size, handoff, `assigned_vendor_slug`, status, attribution. Auto-recorded on `/rent/confirm`; vendor stamped on ACCEPT (inbound webhook) **and** on the Reassign button. **Auto-maintained — no sync needed.**
2. **Unified calendar** (`src/data/rentals-calendar.ts` + `/wheelhouse/rentals/page.tsx`) — reads BOTH streams, grouped by date, each row shows source badge / customer (first+last initial) / item / dates / vendor / status.
3. **Actions** (`/api/wheelhouse/rentals` + `RentalRow.tsx`) — **Send update** (SMS the assigned vendor for THAT booking only — scoped: one rental → one vendor → their phones; a vendor never receives another's bookings even on overlapping dates) + **Reassign** (cart).

Linked from wheelhouse **Tools → "Rentals calendar."**
