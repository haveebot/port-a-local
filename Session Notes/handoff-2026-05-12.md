## Beach booking UX — three layered changes

### 1. Single-day default (PR #43)

Form's date inputs collapsed from **"Start Date + End Date (both required, end > start)"** to **"Setup Date" alone + a multi-day opt-in toggle**. Bron's pattern.

- Single-day mode submits `returnDate = pickupDate` so the API contract stays uniform
- Multi-day toggle reveals "Last Day" input + "← Single day instead" reverse toggle
- Stripe line_item description renders cleanly in both shapes (no `May 24 → May 24` awkwardness)

### 2. /rent hub (PR #44)

Nav-level fix: main "Rentals" link landed on a cart-only page, hiding the beach booking at `/beach` from anyone who didn't know it was there. `/rent` now leads with a neutral "Rentals" hero + two commanding tiles (Carts + Beach Setups). Cart-specific content stays below the tiles for anyone who picks Carts.

### 3. Inclusive day count fix (PR #45)

Surfaced LIVE by Claire's screenshot at 5/11 19:23 UTC: she selected May 23 → May 24 expecting 2 days of beach service, but the form showed "× 1 day × $85 = $170" because the `numDays` calc was interval-based (May 24 minus May 23 = 1 day interval). Now inclusive: May 23 → May 24 = 2 days. Pre-existing bug from the old check-in/check-out paradigm; the single-day shift made it clearly wrong.
