### Twilio newline bug — re-confirmed

PAL's Vercel env `TWILIO_ACCOUNT_SID` and `TWILIO_AUTH_TOKEN` have a literal `\n` baked into the value. Next.js `process.env.*` handles it fine (PAL has been sending SMS all this time), but raw shell parsing breaks. Strip with `sed 's/\\n$//'`. Worth a future env-rotation sweep to clean up all such tokens.

### Civicweb access wall

The City moved newer packets to civicweb's portal which auth-walls PDFs (returns sign-in HTML). The agenda HTML at `/document/<id>/` is publicly readable, sufficient for verbatim resolution titles. AIR PDFs (staff memos) + minutes require a civicweb account (no equivalent to Vercel-style SSO). The 2019 packet is freely downloadable at cityofportaransas.org/wp-content/uploads — newer years moved to civicweb. **This is a journalism-of-record structural finding** that landed in the Dispatch piece's "What the public packet does not contain" section.
